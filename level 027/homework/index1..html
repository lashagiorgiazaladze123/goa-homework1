Overview

The Nissan GT-R R34 is an iconic sports car produced by Nissan from 1999 to 2002. It gained popularity for its performance and appearance, becoming a legend among car enthusiasts worldwide.

Specifications

Engine: 2.6 L RB26DETT twin-turbocharged inline-six

Horsepower: Approximately 280-330 hp (varies by model)

Transmission: 6-speed manual

0-60 mph: Approximately 4.9 seconds
